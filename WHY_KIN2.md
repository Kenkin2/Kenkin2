# Why Kin2

## The problem

UK shift-based businesses — security firms, care providers, cleaning companies, hospitality groups — manage their workforces across too many disconnected tools. A rota in a spreadsheet. Clock-in on a separate app. Timesheets approved via email. Payroll calculated in yet another system. Compliance tracked on paper.

This fragmentation creates:
- Missed shifts and uncovered positions
- Payroll errors and wage underpayments (with HMRC liability)
- GDPR exposure from unmanaged personal data
- Hours lost every week to admin tasks that should take minutes
- No clear view of who is working, where, when, or at what cost

## What Kin2 does

Kin2 Workforce brings every workforce operation into one platform — designed specifically for UK shift-based SMEs.

| Problem | Kin2 Solution |
|---|---|
| Rota planning across WhatsApp and spreadsheets | Drag-and-drop shift builder with mobile publishing |
| No visibility of who clocked in where | GPS-verified clock-in with geofence alerts |
| Timesheets approved via email chains | Digital approval workflows with overtime tracking |
| Wage underpayments and NLW risk | Real-time NLW/NMW enforcement at April 2026 rates |
| HMRC RTI submitted manually | Direct FPS/EPS submission from the platform |
| IR35 and AWR compliance done manually | Built-in assessment tools with audit trails |
| Staff cover arranged by phone | SMS/WhatsApp shift-fill automation with one-tap acceptance |
| No integration with enterprise identity | SAML 2.0 SSO for Growth and Scale customers |

## Why it matters now

UK employment law is getting more complex — IR35, AWR, TUPE, NLW rate changes every April, GDPR enforcement. The businesses that manage shift-based workforces are often the least equipped to handle this complexity.

Kin2 is purpose-built for them. Not adapted from a generic HR tool. Not a spreadsheet plugin. A platform that understands the real operational cadence of a shift-based business.
