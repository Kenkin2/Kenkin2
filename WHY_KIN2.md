# Why Kin2

## What Kin2 is trying to do

Kin2 Workforce is designed to give UK shift-based organisations one place to manage core workforce operations.

## Why it matters

Many shift-based businesses rely on disconnected tools for:
- rotas
- attendance
- timesheets
- staff coordination
- compliance workflows
- payroll-support tasks

Kin2 is intended to bring those workflows together in one product.

## Current strengths

- clear focus on shift-based operations
- public status and roadmap visibility
- practical workforce workflow coverage
- growing public trust and documentation layer
