# Use Cases

## Security companies

Kin2 Workforce can support shift scheduling, attendance tracking, timesheets, and staff coordination for security teams.

## Care providers

Kin2 Workforce can support rota planning, attendance workflows, staff coordination, and related operational tracking for care teams.

## Cleaning businesses

Kin2 Workforce can support scheduling, attendance, timesheets, and multi-site workforce coordination for cleaning operations.

## Hospitality teams

Kin2 Workforce can support rota changes, attendance tracking, and shift-based staff operations for hospitality businesses.

## Charities and community organisations

Kin2 Workforce can support workforce coordination and operational tracking for organisations running shift-based services.
