# Benefits

## Why teams may use Kin2 Workforce

Kin2 Workforce is intended to help shift-based organisations by making key workforce tasks easier to manage in one place.

## Potential benefits

- clearer rota and shift planning
- simpler attendance tracking
- more structured timesheet workflows
- better visibility across workforce operations
- fewer disconnected tools for day-to-day admin
- clearer routes to demo, documentation, and support
