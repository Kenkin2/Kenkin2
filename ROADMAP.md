# Roadmap

## Current direction

Kin2 Workforce is currently focused on strengthening its enterprise platform, expanding developer integration capabilities, and rolling out compliance automation features.

## In progress (Q2 2026)

- Demand Forecasting — machine learning for staffing predictions
- RTW Document AI — automated right-to-work verification processing
- Fraud Detection — anomaly detection across payroll and timekeeping
- Multi-location Dashboard — unified view across all business locations
- Webhooks / Automation — event-driven integrations and workflows
- Manager Self-Service Portal — delegated admin functions

## Near-term focus (Q2–Q3 2026)

- Predictive Absence Alerts — forecasting worker absences to enable proactive scheduling
- FCA Payment Institution application — direct payment capability for workers
- HMRC Direct Submission — automatic payroll data submission to tax authorities
- Enhanced Compliance Reporting — automated incident and grievance tracking

## Medium-term vision (Q4 2026–Q1 2027)

- Platform Economics Dashboard — visibility into platform sustainability and worker earnings
- Humanitarian Support System — safety-gated resources for vulnerable workers
- Provider Marketplace expansion — partner ecosystem for extended functionality
- Post-quantum Cryptography — forward-secure data protection

## Note

This is a public-facing roadmap summary and not a full internal delivery board. For detailed timelines and feature status, contact our sales team.
