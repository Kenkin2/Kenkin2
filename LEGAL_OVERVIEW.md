# Legal Overview

Kin2 Workforce is a software platform that supports workforce operations and compliance workflows for UK shift-based businesses.

## Important positioning

Unless expressly agreed in writing, Kin2 Workforce does not provide:
- legal advice
- tax advice
- immigration advice
- regulated payroll bureau services

## Decision-support wording

Features such as payroll-support calculations, right-to-work document handling, AWR tracking, IR35 support, working-time prompts, fraud alerts, and AI-assisted recommendations are decision-support tools.

Customers remain responsible for human review and for final employment, payroll, tax, immigration, worker-status, and compliance decisions.
